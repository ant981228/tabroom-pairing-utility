Bookmarklet Manager

How to Use This Tool

  1. For each category below, enter team or judge names exactly as they appear in the tournament entries. Hit "Add" after each entry.
  2. Top Teams: Enter the best teams in the field.
  3. Comparable Teams: Enter teams of similar skill level to your own.
  4. Our Teams: Enter your school's teams.
  5. Our Judges: Enter your school's judges.
  6. After adding all entries, click the "Generate Bookmarklet" button.
  7. Only after generating the bookmarklet, follow the instructions below to add it to your browser.
Highlight Teams

How to Add the Bookmarklet to Your Browser

  1. Right-click on the "Highlight Teams" button above
  2. Select "Copy link address" from the context menu
  3. Right-click on your bookmarks bar
  4. Select "Add page" or "Add bookmark"
  5. In the "Name" field, enter a name for your bookmarklet (e.g., "Highlight Teams")
  6. In the "URL" field, paste the copied link address
  7. Click "Save" or "Add"

Note: If you can't see your bookmarks bar, use Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac) to show it.
